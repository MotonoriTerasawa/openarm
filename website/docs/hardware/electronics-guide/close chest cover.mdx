# Close chest cover

1. Put Chest-cover_A from front.

<img
  src="/img/hardware/electronics-guide/chest_cover_closing_front.png"
  alt="chest cover closing front"
  width="80%"
/>

  
2. Put Chest_cover_B from back. Lead J2 and J3-J4 cables to Chest-cover_B's U-shape slot.

<img
  src="/img/hardware/electronics-guide/chest_cover_closing_back.png"
  alt="chest cover closing back"
  width="80%"
/>

3. Use 6 screws to close chest cover. Be careful not to pinch communication cables or extension cables between body and chest cover.

<img
  src="/img/hardware/electronics-guide/chest_cover_screw.png"
  alt="chest cover screw"
  width="80%"
/>
